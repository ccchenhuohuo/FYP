1. Introduction

Welcome to the Stock Trading System. We highly value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy aims to explain how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

2. Information Collection

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Identification Information: Such as name, email address, phone number, etc.
  • Account Information: Such as username, password, etc.
  • Transaction Information: Such as stock trading records, simulated investment data, etc.
  • Device Information: Such as IP address, browser type, operating system, etc.
  • Usage Data: Such as page visit records, feature usage, etc.

3. Information Use

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services.
  • Create and maintain your account.
  • Process your transaction requests.
  • Send you service-related notifications.
  • Prevent fraud and enhance system security.
  • Analyze user behavior to improve user experience.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent.
  • With third-party service providers necessary to provide the service.
  • To comply with legal requirements, court orders, or government agency requests.
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety, and the rights, property, or safety of our users or the public.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no internet transmission or electronic storage method is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of information.

6. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you may have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You have the right to restrict or object to our processing of your personal information.
  • Right to Data Portability: You have the right to receive your data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You have the right to withdraw your consent at any time.
  • Right to Complain: You have the right to lodge a complaint with the relevant data protection authority.

7. Cookie Policy

We use cookies and similar technologies to collect and store information about your use of our services. You can refuse or delete cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect the availability of certain features.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If there are significant changes, we will notify you through website notifications or email.
